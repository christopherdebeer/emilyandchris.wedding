Travel

Old Forest School is about a 40 minute drive from Tauranga, Rotorua and Whakatane at 795 Rotoehu Road, Pongakawa, Bay of Plenty, New Zealand.

Visa Requirements

All South African visitors to New Zealand will require visitor visas from the 21 November this year.

Immigration NZ recommends that applicants apply for a visitor visa about six weeks in advance of their intended travel dates. There is a bit of administration involved in compiling evidence so please ensure you get organized to avoid disappointment. (mainly ours)

Information: https://www.immigration.govt.nz/about-us/media-centre/news-notifications/visa-changes-for-south-african-nationals

Form: https://www.immigration.govt.nz/new-zealand-visas/apply-for-a-visa/tools-and-information/forms-and-guides/visit

Getting to the venue

We are chartering a bus to collect guests from a central Mount Maunganui location, drive them to the venue and return them at the end of the evening. The venue is in a rural location and we want to ensure our guests arrive on time and return home safely so we encourage you to take this option. Please confirm if you would like to reserve a seat on the bus as seats are limited and we don’t want anyone left behind.

By Car

If you are driving, make sure you take a printed copy of travel directions with you as mobile coverage can be patchy in the area and some GPS will take you straight past it.

Easy Directions To Old Forest School from Tauranga/Mount Maunganui

The FAST option (about 10 minutes quicker than below), does have a $2 toll road though. You can pay the toll at www.tollroad.govt.nz within 5 days after you’ve used it.

  1. Head east on the Tauranga Eastern Link (the big new highway connecting Mount Maunganui and Paengaroa).
  2. When the Tauranga Eastern Link ends, at the Paengaroa roundabout, take the first exit on the left, heading towards Whakatane.
  3. You will go over a railway line and past the Te Puke golf course on your left.
  4. Turn right at Pongakawa Station Rd, (its the road after Pongakawa School Road on your right and Weallans Ground Spread on your left).
  5. Pongakawa Station Rd has a derelict old store on the corner.
  6. Old Forest School is approximately 13km from here.
  7. If you get to the BP you have just missed the turn off, back round you go…
  8. Pongakawa Station Rd ‘becomes’ Old Coach Rd and then Rotoehu Rd, so long as you keep to the median strip you will be fine.
  9. You will go over a one way bridge, head past lots of farms.
  10. You’ll pass an Old Forest School sign then go into the Rotoehu Forest.
  11. Look out for the fire station on your left, not far now…….we are on your left and have a sign out front and a blue letterbox.

By Taxi

Taxis to the venue should cost approximately:

  1. $135 From central Tauranga to venue
  2. $125 from central Mount Maunganui to venue
  3. $50 from Pukehina to venue
  4. $80 from Maketu to venue

Due to the rural location (and cost) if you plan to catch a taxi we would suggest booking in advance and ask that you share with other guests. If you plan to catch a taxi in either direction, please let us know so we can attempt to match up people with their locations and desired departure times.
