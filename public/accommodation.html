Accommodation

We will be staying in the Mount Maunganui area between 9th and 13th March & this is the area that the wedding bus will depart from.

We would like to see as much of people as possible during this time (wedding duties permitting!) and hope to plan other activities and catch-ups in the days surrounding the wedding for those that are around.

There are quite a few accommodation options in the area. We have listed a few of them below.

If you fancy staying somewhere more remote, Pukehina Beach is about 12 minutes drive from Old Forest School. It’s a quintessential kiwi holiday beach. If you do stay there (or even if you don’t) make sure you check out the local pub Hippy Pipi, it’s a great place for breakfast too.
