Things To Do

If you’re in the area for a few days, here are a few things worth checking out.

Mount Maunganui
Summit Track

Anytime

A fairly easy 30-40 minute walk up the Mount Maunganui summit track has the most stunning views.

Mount Maunganui (map)
Tauranga, 3116

Dinner In The Domain

Thursday 9th March @ 5.30pm

Dinner In The Domain is a weekly food market with a difference. Providing you with a selection of only the best food trucks, coupled with top local chefs performing live food demonstrations, as well as a licensed area so you can wash it all down with a nice glass of locally brewed beer.

Papamoa Beach Road (map)
Papamoa,
Bay Of Plenty

Street Food Union

Friday 10th March @5.00pm-8pm

Serving up a delicious selection of street food every Friday night, 5pm til 8pm, next to The Rising Tide.

It's the fully licensed, family friendly event, with great music you won't want to miss!

Soper Reserve (map)
95 Newton Street
Mount Maunganui, 3116

Mount Hot Pools

Live Music: March 12, 2017 - from 7pm - 9pm

Open: Mon to Sat 6.00am – 10.00pm
Sun & Public Holidays 8.00am – 10.00pm

Kick back and soak in the Mount Hot Pools while listening to live local acoustic musicians perform. What better way to finish the weekend!

Mount Hot Pools (map)
9 Adams Avenue,
Mount Maunganui, 3116
